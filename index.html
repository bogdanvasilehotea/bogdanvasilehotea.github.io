About Me

Hi! I'm an instructional designer specializing in creating captivating eLearning content and user-friendly interfaces. I'm also on a journey to master game design and Unreal Engine development, having already crafted some cute little games and some beautiful environments.

In the realm of eLearning, I excel in production and development, using tools like Articulate Storyline to design interactive learning experiences that make complex topics easy to grasp. On the game design front, my passion for creating immersive experiences has led me to build beautiful environments and engaging gameplay mechanics.

What I find fascinating is how these seemingly unrelated fields synergize. My instructional design skills enhance user interfaces and overall user experience in game design, while my game design expertise adds excitement and interactivity to eLearning projects, making learning more engaging.

I believe design is all about clear communication, and that merging instructional design principles with the immersive elements of game design will revolutionize education.

  • Whispering Creek

    Unreal Engine environment project

    This was an environment study I did which turned into me attempting to recreate an old hay shed (soproaica) that my grandfather once built. He built it on a hill, but I decided to put my own spin on it and placed it near a peaceful creek instead.

    I had an absolute blast working on this project! I got to play around with different elements like different types of foliage, planar refractions, and material painting. I also added a little bridge across the creek, leading right to the hay shed. It was a fun challenge to capture the rustic vibe and bring back those nostalgic memories of my grandfather's creation.

  • Shrinking Giants

    eLearning project

    Shrinking Giants is a short and informative eLearning course that takes you on a captivating exploration of Hațeg Island, a secluded landmass that existed approximately 70 million years ago in what is now Western Romania.

    Designed for curious learners of all backgrounds, this course unveils the remarkable story of the discovery of miniature dinosaurs on Hațeg Island and delves into their evolutionary journey. From the initial surprise of finding tiny dinosaur fossils to the profound impact on scientific understanding, learners will uncover the secrets of this unique ecosystem.

    With interactive elements such as drag and drop interactions, click-to-reveal interactions, scenario-based learning, and a quiz, this course is suitable for general audiences interested in paleontology, prehistoric life, and the wonders of our planet's history.

  • Cybersecurity 101

    eLearning project

    The Cybersecurity eLearning course is designed for employees of an accounting firm, providing them with essential knowledge and skills to navigate the ever-growing threat landscape of cybersecurity.

    This course aims to equip participants with the principles and best practices necessary to safeguard sensitive data, protect against common cyber threats, and mitigate risks. Targeted towards accounting professionals, this interactive course covers topics such as phishing attacks, malware infections, social engineering, and scenario-based learning.

    Participants will engage in click-to-reveal interactions, a final quiz, and real-life problem-solving exercises to reinforce their understanding and practical application of cybersecurity principles. By completing this course, employees will contribute to maintaining the security of the accounting firm and protect confidential information from potential breaches.

  • Interviewing Skills for Recruiters

    eLearning project

    This eLearning course, "Interviewing Skills for Recruiters," is designed to equip recruiters with the essential knowledge and techniques needed to conduct effective interviews and make informed hiring decisions.

    Ideal for HR professionals, talent acquisition specialists, and recruitment teams, this course provides valuable insights into the interview process. Learners will discover key interview types, appropriate and inappropriate questions to ask, and how to initiate contact with potential candidates. They will also gain an understanding of legal and ethical considerations in interviewing.

    Through interactive slides, scenario-based learning, and practical tips, this course empowers recruiters to enhance their interviewing skills, identify top talent, and contribute to successful organizational outcomes.
